FAQ

What does this app do?

Millions of qualified citizens do not vote in state elections. With nearly anonymous candidates making decisions about education policies/constitutions/etc. Bills are being passed without the approval of our citizens.

This app will help bring information about candidates to your fingertips, so that all you have to do to make a difference in your community is register and go. You can view who is running, who is the current position holder, their basic biographical information, their viewpoints, election dates, and more.

What do I need to be eligible to vote?

  • You must be 18 or older (if you're 17, you can pre-register!)
  • You must be free of a felony/misdemeanor, depending on the laws in your state.
More information about voting

How do I register to vote?

If you meet the qualifications listed above, you can go to your local DMV or government office to register, or you can find the forms online to print below:

Registration forms

On average, registration takes place 30 days before your local elections take place

You must provide photo ID and proof of residency.

Where is my local poll?

Once registration is complete, your poll location will be listed on your registration card.

What are the laws in my state?

If you need more information about the laws in your state to better make a decision about the bills your favourite candidate supports, click your state below.
